US Hospital Facility Bed Capacity Map

Open map data on all US hospitals’ bed capacity to care for COVID-19 patients by facility, county, hospital referral region, or state. See our project for methods, sources, data, and code—all free an open source.

Numbers are not reported in real time. Most are from 2018 facility reports or prior and may be incomplete or outdated: Definitive Healthcare & Healthcare Cost Report Information System (HCRIS). With your help, we are updating this data to show a more current view.
